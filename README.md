## 🔐 Data Privacy & Account Deletion
In accordance with Google Play’s Data Safety policies, **RawVerdict** is committed to user privacy and data control. 

Users can request the permanent deletion of their account and all associated personal data (such as email address, profile information, and app activity) at any time.

### How to Request Deletion:
To submit a formal request for account or data removal, please visit our web-based portal:
👉 **[RawVerdict Account Deletion Request Form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe0Z5ajKRGOTKSrneVCliUNqhGZKYx-KKR413u6sLriz0w3IA/viewform?usp=sf_link)**

**Note:** * Requests are typically processed within **30 days**. 
* You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been permanently removed.
* Some data may be retained for a limited period if required for legal or regulatory compliance.
